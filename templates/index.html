Denmark's Riso Association

Copenhagen, Denmark

The purpose of our association is to bring together all those interested in printing Riso and other related activities, and in the association to create the ability and desire to take responsibility for one's own life and participate actively and engaged in community life. We do that, among other things by creating togetherness, well-being and joy in joint activities and by giving the members permission to publish their own stories and works. It is through creativity and allowing people to use their voice that people are given the opportunity to actively participate in the community and society as a whole.

Our community is on discord and instagram.
